Professional Summary

A concise overview of my background and what I bring to a data science & NLP team

I am a data scientist and computational linguist with a strong foundation in mathematics and engineering, passionate about building intelligent systems that deliver measurable impact. My work spans end-to-end data workflows, from designing and deploying machine learning pipelines to creating intuitive analytics dashboards for decision-makers. I have engineered solutions that cut government document review time from hours to minutes, optimized workforce allocation for regional service providers, and developed natural language processing applications ranging from biomedical summarization to sentiment analysis.

Proficient in Python, SQL, R, and modern ML/NLP frameworks, I combine technical expertise with a collaborative, problem-solving mindset. My experience includes prototyping a data pipeline for the BC Ministry of Environment, business intelligence models for The Byng Group, and LLM-driven applications leveraging Hugging Face, Ollama, and retrieval-augmented generation. I thrive in environments that value innovation, adaptability, and real-world impact, and I approach every project with a balance of analytical rigour and user-focused design.
